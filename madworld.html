Mad World

Welcome!
By: Kevin Soria
Mad World is a story about a 16 year old girl, named Hayato Kuro; a girl who made a contract with the demon, Rangda. She is what you would call a, 'Demon Contractor', a human who makes a contract with a demon. With this newfound powers, she joins a sect of the church who pays these, 'Demon Contractors' to eradicate demons who are too strong to be exorcised.
